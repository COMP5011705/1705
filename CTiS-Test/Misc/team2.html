Ethical issues relating to the technology, your personal reflections and recommendations

After researching on the topic at hand, I have learned a lot about cybersecurity. I have learnt it's significance
and how valuable it is with the rising threats of cyberattacks. With technology evolving every day and having
different uses and different purposes, if fallen into the wrong hands it could cause major damage. This research
has taught me how I can protect myself from such attacks and how to act if I ever to fall into one of these unfortunate
events. The same way I feel it is very beneficial for other to learn of the importance of cybersecurity and adopt it’s
values in their lives. Businesses would benefit greatly from implementing good cybersecurity practices. By having robust
firewalls and protocols and educating their colleagues on how to treat such attacks, they would benefit in the work rate and
efficiency at which work is done. At the same time all the advancements made can also be a downfall as I have reflected on
in my choices sections that AI is a strong defence against attacks, but if the same AI were accessed by someone intending to
threaten the company, they would have access to a lot of information. They could also alter this information to their benefit.
With cloud computing , people must be careful what they put up on the cloud and should also have good protection as this
valuable data can be accessed by skilled hackers. Out of all the topics I have spoken about, I agree with the fact that higher
education of cybersecurity needs to be implemented to the society and more importantly kids, as they are the future, and it is
always good to have them understand and see cybersecurity’s importance from a young age. This in turn would give us more people
applying for cybersecurity related jobs and in turn help society be more protected from cyber threats.
